## 2024-05-18 - Hardcoded Fallback Secrets & Mass Assignment Vulnerabilities
**Vulnerability:** Found hardcoded fallback secrets in `config.js` (`MONGO_URL`) and `UserController.js`/`authMiddleware.js` (`JWT_SECRET`). Found a mass assignment vulnerability in `UserController.js` `registerUser` where `role`, `department`, and `skills` were extracted from `req.body` and set to the user document, allowing privilege escalation.
**Learning:** Hardcoded secrets existed due to reliance on convenience fallbacks (`process.env.SECRET || 'secret123'`) for local development, which compromises the production environment if the environment variable is missing. The mass assignment existed due to un-validated destructuring of `req.body` and passing it directly to `User.create()`.
**Prevention:** Avoid convenience fallbacks for sensitive configuration. Explicitly fail securely (`process.exit(1)` or `throw/500`) when environment secrets are missing. Do not rely on `req.body` defaults during resource creation; strictly validate or hardcode sensitive fields.
